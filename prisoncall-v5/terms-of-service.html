2. Description of Service

Prisoncall Australia provides subscribers with a local landline phone number that receives incoming calls from correctional facilities and forwards them to the subscriber's nominated Australian mobile number. This allows family members and other approved contacts to receive calls from inmates at local call rates.

The service does not place outgoing calls and does not provide any connection to correctional facility systems beyond standard number forwarding.

The Prisoncall service is currently available for Victorian and New South Wales correctional facilities only. Coverage of specific facilities is subject to change. It is your responsibility to confirm that your loved one's facility is supported before purchase.
