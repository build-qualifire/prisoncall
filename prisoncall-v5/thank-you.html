Important tips

  • Check your SMS and email, your Prisoncall number will arrive within minutes

  • Turn off voicemail temporarily, the prison may call to verify your number

  • Answer all unknown incoming calls, it could be the prison calling to verify

  • Save your Prisoncall number in your contacts. When the prison officer calls to verify, they will ask you to confirm your number. Quote your Prisoncall number, not your personal mobile number.
